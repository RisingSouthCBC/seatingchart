Instructions

  • Enter a name and click "Add Person" to add a new person
  • Drag and drop people from the unassigned list to seats
  • Drag people between seats to rearrange
  • Double-click a seat to remove the person
  • Use "Update Grid" to change the seating layout
  • Use "Save Chart" to save your arrangement (downloads as JSON)
  • Use "Load Chart" to restore a saved arrangement
